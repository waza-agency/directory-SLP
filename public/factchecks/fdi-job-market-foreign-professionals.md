### CLAIM 10: BMW Plant Employment & Opening

> "2,500+ direct employees at San Luis Potosí plant; Opened 2019"

**VERDICT:** OUTDATED

**Investigation Summary:**
The 2019 opening date is correct, but the employment figure is outdated—current employment is approximately 3,700.

**Evidence Chain:**
- [BMW Group Plants - San Luis Potosí](https://www.bmwgroup-werke.com/san-luis-potosi/en.html)
- [BMW Group Press](https://www.press.bmwgroup.com/usa/article/detail/T0296856EN_US/bmw-group-opens-plant-in-san-luis-potosi-mexico)
- [BMW Group Careers](https://www.bmwgroup.jobs/mx/en/location/location-sanluispotosi.html)

**Detailed Analysis:**
- **Opening date**: Confirmed. Pre-production started April 2019; official inauguration ceremony in June 2019 with investment of over US$1 billion.
- **Employment**: The 2,500 figure was accurate at opening. However, **current employment is around 3,700 employees** who mainly produce the BMW 3 Series, BMW 2 Series Coupé, and BMW M2.
- **Future expansion**: BMW is investing another 800 million euros for NEUE KLASSE electric vehicle production starting 2027.

**Confidence:** High - Original data was accurate; needs updating to current figures.

### CLAIM 12: Industrial Engineer Salary Ranges

> "Entry: $15,000-$18,000; Mid: $22,000-$30,000; Senior: $35,000-$45,000 MXN monthly"

**VERDICT:** PARTIALLY TRUE

**Investigation Summary:**
Salary ranges are reasonable but vary significantly by source and specific industry sector.

**Evidence Chain:**
- [Data México - Industrial Engineers](https://www.economia.gob.mx/datamexico/en/profile/occupation/ingenieros-industriales)
- [Glassdoor - Industrial Engineer Mexico](https://www.glassdoor.com/Salaries/mexico-industrial-engineer-salary-SRCH_IL.0,6_IN169_KO7,26.htm)
- [Salary Explorer - Engineering Mexico](https://www.salaryexplorer.com/average-salary-wage-comparison-mexico-engineering-c139f22)

**Detailed Analysis:**
Government data shows average industrial engineer salary around **$7,930 MXN monthly** nationally, which is significantly lower than the blog's entry-level claim. However, high-paying sectors (automotive repair, specialized industries) report salaries up to $30,000 MXN monthly. The blog's figures appear to represent **manufacturing sector positions in industrial zones** rather than national averages.

**Confidence:** Medium - Ranges are plausible for specific sectors but higher than national averages.
