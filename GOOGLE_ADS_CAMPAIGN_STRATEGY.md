### Primary Personas

**Persona 1: The Planning Expat**
- Age: 28-45
- Location: USA, Canada, Europe (currently)
- Intent: Researching relocation to Mexico
- Pain Points: Cost concerns, safety questions, finding services in English

## Risk Mitigation & Contingency Plans

### Risk 1: High CPC in Competitive Keywords

**Mitigation:**
- Start with long-tail, low-competition keywords
- Focus on geo-targeted terms (city-specific)
- Use phrase/exact match to avoid broad traffic
- Monitor Quality Score closely (aim for 7+)

**Contingency:**
- If CPC >$2.00 for low-value traffic, pause keyword
- Shift budget to better-performing campaigns
- Focus on SEO for high-CPC keywords instead

---

### Risk 2: Low Conversion Rate from Ads

**Mitigation:**
- Optimize landing pages BEFORE scaling budget
- Clear, compelling CTAs on every page
- A/B test forms, headlines, and value props
- Implement exit-intent popups
- Use remarketing aggressively

**Contingency:**
- If conversion rate <2% after Month 1, pause campaigns
- Conduct user testing on landing pages
- Revise value proposition and messaging
- Consider offering lead magnet (free guide) to lower barrier

---

### Risk 3: Insufficient Business Subscription Signups

**Mitigation:**
- Offer 3-month free trial to first 20 businesses
- Provide white-glove onboarding (do setup for them)
- Create case studies showing ROI
- Guarantee minimum inquiries or refund
- Partner with local business associations

**Contingency:**
- If <2 subs/month after Month 2, revise pricing
- Consider freemium model (free basic listing, paid premium)
- Add more value (featured blog posts, social media promotion)
- Pivot to direct advertising model instead of subscriptions

---

### Risk 4: Market Too Small (Not Enough Search Volume)

**Mitigation:**
- Expand targeting to all of Mexico (broader "expat Mexico" terms)
- Target "considering moving to Mexico" earlier in funnel
- Create content to rank organically for low-volume keywords
- Expand to Display Network and YouTube for awareness

**Contingency:**
- If traffic <1,500 clicks/month after optimization, reduce budget
- Focus on SEO and content marketing instead
- Build email list and community (owned media)
- Monetize through affiliate partnerships instead of ads
