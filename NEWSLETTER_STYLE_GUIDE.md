## 1. Newsletter Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | San Luis Way Weekly |
| **Frequency** | Weekly (sent every Monday morning) |
| **Target Audience** | Expats, tourists, repatriados, and locals interested in San Luis PotosÃ­ |
| **Primary Goal** | Give readers a complete picture of the Potosino scene: news, events, lifestyle, and local insights |
| **Tone** | Friendly, informative, conversational - like a knowledgeable friend updating you on the week |
| **Language** | English (primary), with Spanish terms where culturally appropriate |

### Newsletter Philosophy

The San Luis Way Weekly is NOT just an events calendar. It's a **weekly digest of Potosino life** that helps readers:
- Stay informed about what's happening in the city and region
- Discover events worth attending
- Understand local news that affects daily life
- Learn about the culture, people, and places that make SLP unique
- Feel connected to the community even if they're new to the city

## 5. HTML Email Design Specifications

### Brand Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary (Gold) | `#FFCB05` | Headers, buttons, accents |
| Secondary (Terracotta) | `#C75B39` | Section backgrounds, highlights |
| Dark Gray | `#1F2937` | Body text |
| Light Gray | `#F3F4F6` | Section backgrounds |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Card backgrounds |
| Link Blue | `#2563EB` | Links |

### Typography

| Element | Font | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| Header/Logo | Georgia, serif | 28px | Bold |
| Section Titles | Arial, sans-serif | 20px | Bold |
| Subheadings | Arial, sans-serif | 16px | Bold |
| Body Text | Arial, sans-serif | 15px | Normal |
| Small Text | Arial, sans-serif | 13px | Normal |
| Footer | Arial, sans-serif | 12px | Normal |

### Design Principles

- **Max width:** 600px (standard for email)
- **Mobile responsive:** Stack columns on mobile
- **Padding:** 20-30px sections, 15px content
- **Border radius:** 8px for cards
- **Line height:** 1.6 for readability
- **Images:** Always include alt text, max-width: 100%

## 8. Writing Style Guidelines

### Voice & Tone

- **Conversational:** Write like you're updating a friend, not writing a news report
- **Helpful:** Always think "how does this help the reader?"
- **Inclusive:** Remember readers range from new arrivals to long-term residents
- **Balanced:** Mix serious news with fun discoveries
- **Local but accessible:** Use Spanish terms but explain them

### Writing Rules

**DO:**
- Use **active voice** and present tense
- Keep sentences **short** (15-20 words max)
- Include **specific details** (dates, addresses, prices)
- Use **bullet points** for scanning
- Add **emojis** to mark sections (not overuse)
- Explain **Spanish terms** briefly
- Include **"why it matters"** for news items
- Write for **5th-grade reading level**

**DON'T:**
- Write walls of text
- Assume local knowledge
- Be alarmist about safety/crime
- Overload with too many items
- Forget links to sanluisway.com
- Use complex jargon
- Be overly promotional

