## 6. Writing Style Guidelines

### Voice & Tone

- **Conversational:** Write like you're updating a friend, not writing a news report
- **Helpful:** Always think "how does this help the reader?"
- **Inclusive:** Remember readers range from new arrivals to long-term residents
- **Balanced:** Mix serious news with fun discoveries
- **Local but accessible:** Use Spanish terms but explain them

### Writing Rules

**DO:**
- Use **active voice** and present tense
- Keep sentences **short** (15-20 words max)
- Include **specific details** (dates, addresses, prices)
- Use **bullet points** for scanning
- Add **emojis** to mark sections (not overuse)
- Explain **Spanish terms** briefly
- Include **"why it matters"** for news items
- Write for **5th-grade reading level**

**DON'T:**
- Write walls of text
- Assume local knowledge
- Be alarmist about safety/crime
- Overload with too many items
- Forget links to sanluisway.com
- Use complex jargon
- Be overly promotional
